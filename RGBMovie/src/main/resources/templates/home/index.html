We are developing a unique concept of transforming former multiplexes into “Cultureplex”, where customers not only enjoy the movie, but also have access to diverse entertainment experiences. At CGV we implement the latest technology including SCREENX, IMAX, STARIUM, 4DX, Dolby Atmos as well as completely new and different F&B offerings. All while still providing the highest quality service by RGB Movie's staff.

There’s an old maxim that states, “No fun for the buyer, no fun for the seller.”No matter what industry you’re working in, you should live and die by this statement.

Before you do anything, be sure to pick a movie that actually interests you. Nothing – and I mean NOTHING - will kill a person more effectively than a bad cinema spot. You can tell a place is boring as soon as you walk in, and it’s so cringe-worthy it’s a little embarrassing.

We can hear your objections already. “But dude, I have to watch with my gf/kid(s).” We feel your pain, we really do. During the course of our career, we’ve worked for most of the cinema there are, so we can tell how a bad cinema spot feels. Therefore, in that situation, assure that we'll provide you with the best service, the best experience to ease your pain.
